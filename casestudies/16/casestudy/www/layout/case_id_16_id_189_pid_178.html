Survey

Choose ONLY five topics you would include in the article.

Imagine that you are Jackson Dykman. It is your job to put together a 10-page (5 spreads) graphics story which will be a "mirror on America." Unlike Dykman, you have unlimited time and resources to do this right.

What five topics would you choose? What unifying theme, if any, would tie together these topics?

Use the survey form below to choose your topics. You can use the categories Dykman identified, or create your own. Come to class prepared to argue for your theme.
