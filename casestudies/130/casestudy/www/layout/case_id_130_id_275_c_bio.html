Biography: Doron Isaacs


Doron Isaacs

Doron Isaacs was Equal Education’s deputy general secretary. He helped found the organization and was one of the first two paid staff members when the organization launched on February 1, 2008. Isaacs holds a law degree from the University of Cape Town.
