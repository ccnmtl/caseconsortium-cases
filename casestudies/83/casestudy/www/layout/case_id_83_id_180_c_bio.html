Biography: Ken Schwencke

Ken Schwencke began as an intern at the Los Angeles Times in 2009, and subsequently won a position as a staff Web Producer. Schwencke graduated from the University of Florida with a journalism degree in 2009, and was Online Editor at his college newspaper.
