Biography: Julie Marquis

Julie Marquis in 2010 was the Senior Special Projects Editor, focused on investigative stories.  She joined the Los Angeles Times in 1994. In 2004, she edited “The Troubles at King/Drew” series that profiled a troubled hospital in Los Angeles. The series won the 2005 Pulitzer Prize for Public Service and the Robert F. Kennedy Journalism Award.
