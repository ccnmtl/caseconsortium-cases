Biography: Jessica Logan

Jessica Logan joined the Californian as a reporter in 2004. As court reporter, Logan wrote breaking news, features, and enterprise stories on a variety of topics including criminal, civil, and appeals cases; court administration; and related agencies. She wrote news updates for the Californian’s website and shot, produced, and wrote scripts for the website’s video stories. A graduate of the University of California, Irvine, Logan received her Master’s in Journalism from the University of Southern California in 2002.
