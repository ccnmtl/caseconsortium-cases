Biography: Christine Peterson

Christine Peterson became news editor of the Californian in the summer of 2005 and led a team of reporters, columnists, and assistant editors who reported and wrote news for both the paper’s A1 and local sections. She worked with reporters to write and produce videos, audio reports, podcasts, and continuous news posts for the website.

Prior to becoming news editor, Peterson served as the paper’s business editor. She had also worked as the Californian’s assistant city editor, as a copy editor, and as a staff writer. Before moving to Bakersfield she was a reporter for the Orange County Register. A graduate of Santa Clara University, Peterson twice won the Californian’s Publisher’s Excellence award, as well as awards for breaking news.
