Biography: Nick Davies

Nick Davies had worked for the Guardian since 1979. Besides his freelance journalism, he wrote a book called Flat Earth News , about what he dubbed “churnalism”—the tendency of news organizations to publish stories that are false, distorted or propaganda. In 2009, he broke a story for the Guardian that the News of the World had tapped private phones to get material for articles.
