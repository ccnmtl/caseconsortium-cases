Biography: Donna St. George

Donna St. George was a reporter on the Metro desk at the Washington Post. In 2008, St. George had been a reporter for 25 years. She has worked at the New York Times, the Philadelphia Enquirer, the Los Angeles Times and Newsday. St. George had spent over a decade at the Post where she wrote human interest stories, primarily about family and children’s issues. She had also written extensively about the military.
