Biography: ThomasMucha


Thomas Mucha, editor

Thomas Mucha had a background in international business economics. He spent 10 years in television, holding a variety of jobs at CNN and CNN International in both New York and London. He then earned a master's degree from the University of Chicago before moving into magazines journalism. Mucha joined GlobalPost in 2008 as managing editor, and two years later became editor.
