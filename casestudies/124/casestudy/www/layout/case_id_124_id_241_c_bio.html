Biography: Tatiana Wah

Tatiana Wah was director of the Haiti Research and Policy Program in the Center on Globalization and Sustainable Development at Columbia University's Earth Institute. She advised the Haitian government on policy aimed at achieving the United Nations' Millennium Development Goals.

In 2010, Wah was named Director of Strategy and Planning for the Interim Haiti Recovery Commission (IHRC), which organized the country's recovery from the 7.0 magnitude earthquake that devastated Port-au-Prince in January that year.
