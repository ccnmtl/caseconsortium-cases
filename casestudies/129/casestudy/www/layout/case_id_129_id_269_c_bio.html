Martin Nisenholtz

Martin Nisenholtz was CEO of New York Times Digital from June 1999 to March 2005, and senior vice president of digital operations from March 2005.  He joined the Times in June 1995 as president of its electronic media company. Before that, he had founded the Interactive Marketing Group at the advertising agency Ogilvy and Mather and directed content strategy for telephone company Ameritech.
