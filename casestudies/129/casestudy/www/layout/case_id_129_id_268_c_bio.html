Biography: Rich Meislin


Via Twitter
Rich Meislin

Rich Meislin was associate managing editor for Internet publishing from September 2005 to June 2008, when he took a buyout during the first round of newsroom cuts. He joined the Times in 1975 as a copy boy, and served in many roles including editor of technology news. He was editor-in-chief of New York Times Digital from March 1998 to February 2001.
