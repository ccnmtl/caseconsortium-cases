Jonathan Landman

Jonathan Landman was deputy managing editor for New York Times Digital from September 2005 to October 2009. He joined the Times in 1992 and served in many roles, including deputy Washington editor and metropolitan editor. Before that, he worked for the New York Daily News and Newsday , among others.

Landman discusses his role as the "web guy."
