Andy Rosenthal

Andy Rosenthal became editorial page editor of the New York Times in January 2007. He was deputy editorial page editor from August 2003 to January 2007. Rosenthal joined the New York Times in 1987, and served in many roles including foreign editor, national editor and assistant managing editor.
