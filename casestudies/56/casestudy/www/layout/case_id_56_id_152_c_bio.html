Biography: Lucio Rafael Pagliaro

Director Lucio Rafael Pagliaro began his career as a marketing manager at Clarín newspaper and, along with CEO Héctor Magnetto and Vice President José Antonio Aranda, was a founding member of Grupo Clarín’s executive committee, which steered the newspaper company into new sectors of the Argentine Media. Beginning in the 1990s, Pagliaro took over the management of Grupo Clarín’s television, radio, cable, and entertainment division.
