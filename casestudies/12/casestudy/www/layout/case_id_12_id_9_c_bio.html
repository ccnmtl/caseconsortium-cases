George Stanley is the Milwaukee Journal Sentinel 痴 managing editor預 post he assumed in January 1997. Stanley joined the Milwaukee Sentinel in 1989 as a roving state reporter, covering the environment, agricultural issues, and Indian affairs. He was named business editor of the Milwaukee Sentinel in 1993, and led the merger of the Journal and Sentinel Business News Departments in 1995. Before joining the Sentinel , George was the outdoor and environment writer for the Wichita Eagle in Wichita, Kansas, a senior staff writer at Ducks Unlimited magazine, and assistant editor of the Lake Geneva Regional News . A member of the board of directors for the Associated Press Managing Editors association, and former president of the Wisconsin AP Editors, Stanley graduated from the University of Wisconsin-Madison in 1979 with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Journalism.
