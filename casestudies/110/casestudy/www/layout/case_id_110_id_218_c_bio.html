Biography: Danfung Dennis


Danfung Dennis

Danfung Dennis was a freelance photojournalist who covered the wars in Afghanistan and Iraq for Newsweek, Time, the New York Times, the Washington Post, the Guardian, Rolling Stone and the Wall Street Journal. His documentary film work earned him the Bayeux-Calvados Award For War Correspondents and an Academy Award nomination. He later became CEO and founder of immersive video software startup Condition One.

Dennis discusses what led him to pursue photojournalism.
