Biography: Mike Lerner


Mike Lerner

Mike Lerner was a documentary film producer. He has made films for BBC, Channel Four, Five, ITV, Discovery and the Travel Channel on subjects ranging from Picasso to Afghanistan.

Lerner discusses changes in filmmaking.
