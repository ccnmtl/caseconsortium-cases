Biography: Robert Friedman


 


Robert Friedman
© Forbes

Robert Friedman was an Editor-at-large at Bloomberg News, where he managed long-term features and projects for the global finance team. Prior to joining Bloomberg in April 2008, Friedman was international editor of Fortune. He also helped organize Fortune's Global CEO Forums in Beijing and New Delhi. Friedman was an assistant managing editor of Life magazine from 1995 to 2000, where he was in charge of international and medical coverage.
