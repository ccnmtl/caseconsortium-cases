Biography: Amanda Bennett


Amanda Bennett
Photo © Natasha Cholerton Brown (via Newsworks)

Amanda Bennett led the Bloomberg Projects and Investigations Team. Before joining Bloomberg in 2007, she served as editor of the Philadelphia Inquirer (2003-06), and prior to that was editor of the Herald-Leader in Lexington, Kentucky. She also served for three years as managing editor/projects for the Oregonian in Portland.
