Biography: JohnVoskuhl


John Voskuhl
Photo from John Voskuhl

John Voskuhl was an editor on the Projects & Investigations Team for Bloomberg News . A graduate of the University of Kentucky, as of 2011 he had been a professional journalist for more than 25 years. A resident of South Florida, Voskuhl had previously worked at the Miami Herald , the Lexington Herald-Leader and the (Louisville) Courier-Journal .

Voskuhl on Bloomberg's team of qualified financial journalists.
