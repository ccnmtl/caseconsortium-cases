Biography: PhilKuntz



Phil Kuntz
Bloomberg

Phil Kuntz was an editor-at-large for Bloomberg News, focusedon computer-assisted reporting.Before joining Bloomberg in 2007, he worked at the Wall Street Journal as an investigative reporter, projects editor, assistant Washington bureau chief and deputy editor of the Money & Investing section. For 13 years at the Journal and seven years at Congressional Quarterly , he wrote extensively about campaign finance, lobbying, influence-peddling, government corruption and the financial markets.
