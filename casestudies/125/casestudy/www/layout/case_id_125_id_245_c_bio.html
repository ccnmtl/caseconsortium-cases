Biography: William Miller


Courtesy of William Miller
William Miller

William Miller was appointed executive director of Greater Homewood Community Corporation (GHCC) in Baltimore in 1998, the same year the organization began operating the Experience Corps program. He was still GHCC director when, in 2004 and 2005, researchers at Johns Hopkins University applied for grants from the National Institutes of Health to conduct a five-year randomized controlled trial of Baltimore Experience Corps. Prior to his work at GHCC, Miller directed the Campus Boulevard Corporation, a community revitalization effort in northwest Philadelphia.
