Biography: Gregory Serrao

Gregory Serrao was founder and CEO of American Dental Partners, a Wakefield, Massachusetts company devoted to providing business services to dental groups. A graduate of New York University, Serrao had worked as an investment banker at Dean Witter Reynolds specializing in healthcare, and at Cardinal Health, Inc., a distributor of pharmaceuticals.
